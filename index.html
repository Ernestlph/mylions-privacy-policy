MyLions

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 May 2026

MyLions is a member app for Lions District 308 B5. It helps members discover events, register for events, upload offline transfer receipts, share event Moments, and find other Lions through member directory features.

MyLions does not process online payments, card payments, FPX payments, loans, investments, insurance, or cryptocurrency services. Event receipts are uploaded only as proof of offline transfer or payment for review by authorized officers.

Information We Collect

Depending on your role and app usage, MyLions may collect or store:

  • Account information such as name, email address, username, password credentials, and session tokens.
  • Member profile information such as club, district, membership number, role, phone number, profile visibility, employment, business, and social profile details.
  • Event activity such as event registrations, cancellations, attendance-related records, receipt review status, and committee actions.
  • Uploaded content such as event cover images, Moment images, Moment captions, receipt images, receipt PDFs, reference numbers, notes, and related metadata.
  • App activity such as reactions, reports, moderation actions, notification preferences, and audit records required for member and officer workflows.
  • Technical information needed to operate the app, such as API requests, authentication status, device/network state, and diagnostic logs.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide login, profile, event, receipt, Moment, and member directory features.
  • Show member information according to profile visibility and backend permission rules.
  • Allow authorized officers, committee members, and administrators to review events, receipts, members, and content.
  • Protect the app, investigate abuse, maintain audit records, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Send or display relevant event, receipt, club, and district updates where supported.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Member information may be visible to other members or officers only through the app's intended features and permission rules. Uploaded files and app data may be processed by service providers that host or operate the MyLions backend, database, storage, and related infrastructure.

Data Storage And Security

MyLions uses authentication, role-based access controls, backend permission checks, and private storage for sensitive records such as receipts. We take reasonable steps to protect member data, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Retention And Deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide MyLions features, support club and district operations, maintain audit records, comply with obligations, and resolve disputes. Members may request correction or deletion of their information through the MyLions support or developer contact listed on Google Play. Some records may need to be retained where required for legitimate operational, audit, security, or legal reasons.

Children

MyLions is intended for Lions members and authorized officers. It is not designed for children under 13.

Ads And Tracking

MyLions does not include advertising features in the current Android prototype. If this changes, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, data access requests, correction requests, or deletion requests, please use the developer contact email shown for MyLions in the Google Play listing.

This Privacy Policy applies to the MyLions Android prototype and related MyLions services operated for Lions District 308 B5.
