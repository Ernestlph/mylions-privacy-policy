MyLions Prototype

Child Safety Standards

Effective date: 29 May 2026

MyLions Prototype is a member app for Lions District 308 B5. The app includes member directory, event, receipt, and Moment-sharing features for invited members and authorized officers.

MyLions Prototype prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Content or behavior that exploits, abuses, sexualizes, traffics, grooms, endangers, or solicits a minor is not allowed.

Prohibited Content And Behavior

Users must not use MyLions Prototype to create, upload, share, request, promote, or facilitate:

  • CSAM or any sexual content involving minors.
  • Grooming, sextortion, trafficking, solicitation, or predatory behavior involving minors.
  • Attempts to contact, pressure, exploit, or endanger a minor.
  • Links, instructions, coded language, or off-platform coordination related to CSAE or CSAM.
  • Any content or behavior that violates applicable child safety laws.

Reporting In The App

MyLions Prototype includes in-app reporting for user-generated Moments. Users can report concerning Moment content from the Moment viewer. Reports are routed to authorized reviewers for follow-up. Users may also use the developer contact email shown on the Google Play listing to report urgent child safety concerns.

Review And Enforcement

When MyLions Prototype becomes aware of content or behavior that may violate these standards, authorized reviewers may remove or restrict content, suspend or restrict accounts, preserve relevant records, and escalate the matter as appropriate. Confirmed or suspected CSAM may be reported to relevant regional or national authorities in accordance with applicable law.

Compliance With Child Safety Laws

MyLions Prototype is intended to comply with applicable child safety laws and Google Play child safety requirements. The app is not designed for children under 13, but the standards above apply regardless of whether a child is an intended user.

Child Safety Contact

For child safety notifications from Google Play, enforcement questions, or urgent CSAE/CSAM concerns, use the developer contact email shown for MyLions Prototype in the Google Play listing. The contact must be able to speak about MyLions Prototype child safety procedures and coordinate action when required.

Privacy Policy

For general privacy information, see the MyLions Privacy Policy.

These standards apply to MyLions Prototype and related MyLions services operated for Lions District 308 B5.
